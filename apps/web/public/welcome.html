Central Command

One dashboard for everything you track.

Central Command is a Cloudflare-native personal performance dashboard. It unifies your calendar, weather, fitness, nutrition, sleep, gaming, and news into a single, private interface — so the signal from every part of your day lives in one place.

Open the dashboard → Sign-in required · access is invite-only

Calendar

Upcoming events and a duration-based busyness score for the day.

Weather

Current conditions and forecast at a glance.

Health

Sleep, nutrition, and fitness logging with a daily performance score.

Gaming

Match results and ranked progress, correlated with your habits.

Your Google data

If you connect Google Calendar, Central Command requests read-only access to display your upcoming events and compute your busyness score. It never modifies your calendar, and your data is never sold or used for advertising.

  • Read-only calendar access — display and busyness scoring only
  • Tokens encrypted at rest; calendar data cached only briefly
  • Revoke access any time from your Google Account permissions
